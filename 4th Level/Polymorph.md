> ## Description
> This spell transforms a creature with at least 1 hit point that you can see within range into a new form. An unwilling creature must make a Wisdom saving throw to avoid the effect. A shapechanger automatically succeeds on this saving throw.<br> The transformation lasts for the duration, or until the target drops to 0 hit points or dies.
